SEO in B2B: Do You Really Need It?
In this article you'll find pros and cons of making SEO for B2B and have a look on some data about it
SEO is crucial for B2B businesses for several reasons:


1. Improved visibility: SEO helps your website to rank higher in search engine results pages (SERPs), which means more potential customers can find your business online.


2. Increased credibility: High search engine rankings can also improve your credibility and reputation among potential customers.


3. Cost-effectiveness: Compared to other forms of digital marketing, SEO can provide a higher return on investment over the long term.


4. Competitive advantage: Investing in SEO can give your business a competitive advantage over other B2B companies that are not as focused on optimizing their online presence.


5. Long-term benefits: Unlike other forms of advertising that require ongoing investment, the benefits of SEO can continue to accrue over time, even after you stop actively investing in it.


Overall, SEO is a critical component of any B2B marketing strategy, helping to improve visibility, credibility, and cost-effectiveness while providing a competitive advantage in the marketplace.

According to a study by Google, 89% of B2B researchers use the internet during the research process, with 71% starting their research with a generic search. This highlights the importance of having a strong online presence, including a well-optimized website, to attract and engage potential customers.


Furthermore, a study by BrightEdge found that organic search drives 53% of website traffic, making it the largest source of website traffic for B2B companies. This emphasizes the importance of ranking well in search engine results pages (SERPs) to drive traffic and generate leads.


In addition, a study by HubSpot found that companies that prioritize blogging are 13 times more likely to see a positive return on investment (ROI) from their marketing efforts. This highlights the importance of creating high-quality, optimized content to attract and engage potential customers.


Overall, the data shows that SEO is crucial for B2B businesses to improve their online visibility, drive traffic, generate leads, and ultimately, increase revenue. By investing in SEO, B2B companies can gain a competitive advantage in the marketplace and achieve long-term benefits.
